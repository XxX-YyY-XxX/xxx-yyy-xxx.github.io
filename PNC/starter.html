Can I F2P this game?

Depends on your focus.

In terms of collecting and raising your units, verily. This game runs on a shards system (which are farmable, with a few exceptions), so dupes are pretty much just a collateral.

In terms of skins though, this is pretty much their main source of income. You can still get free skins and a limited amount of free skin currencies.

TLDR; Basically, yes.
